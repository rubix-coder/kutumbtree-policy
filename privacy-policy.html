1. Overview

KutumbTree is a family tree management application that helps you create, organize, and share your family's genealogical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

By using KutumbTree, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Required to create your account and enable login
  • Display Name: The name you choose to display on your profile
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely (never transmitted unencrypted)
  • Phone Number (Optional): If provided, used for account recovery

2.2 Family Tree Data

You may voluntarily provide the following information about family members:

  • Names, dates of birth, and dates of death
  • Gender and relationship information
  • Profile photos and biographical information
  • Family notes and historical records
  • Contact information for family members

2.3 Usage and Device Information

  • Log Data: Server logs recording API requests (timestamps, endpoints, status codes)
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version (for error reporting)
  • Session Information: Authentication tokens for session management
  • Usage Statistics: Features accessed, actions performed, time spent

2.4 Communication Data

  • Emails: Any correspondence with our support team
  • Feedback: Bug reports, feature requests, and surveys

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your account
  • Service Delivery: Providing and improving KutumbTree's features
  • Communication: Sending transactional emails and support responses
  • Security: Preventing fraud and protecting user accounts
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns to improve the service
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory and legal requirements
Note on Railway Hobby Plan: KutumbTree is hosted on Railway's Hobby plan, which may experience cold starts (30-60 second delays when the app hasn't been used). We are transparent about this infrastructure limitation.

4. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion (upon user request)
  • Family Tree Data: Retained until user deletion
  • Log Data: Retained for 30 days for security and debugging purposes
  • Backup Data: May be retained for up to 90 days after deletion for recovery purposes

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure JWT tokens with expiration
  • Database Security: Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular Backups: Automated backups for data recovery
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication when available.

6. Information Sharing

6.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • Third-party marketers or advertisers
  • Data brokers or data aggregators
  • Social media platforms
  • Any external services without your consent

6.2 We MAY Share Data With:

  • Family Members: Only the information you choose to share with them
  • Service Providers: Hosting providers (Railway) and database services (if applicable)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law enforcement or court order
  • Security Threats: To protect against fraud or security threats

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data. Contact us at p.jesal.work@gmail.com with the subject "Data Access Request."

7.2 Rectification

You can update or correct your account information directly in the app settings.

7.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data. Email support@kutumbtree.com with "Account Deletion Request" - we will process this within 30 days.

7.4 Opt-Out

You can opt-out of non-essential email communications by clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email we send.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

KutumbTree is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For users 13-18: If you are under 18, your parents/guardians should review this privacy policy and consent to your use of KutumbTree.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately at p.jesal.work@gmail.com
