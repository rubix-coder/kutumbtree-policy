✅ KutumbTree Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy defines the standards of conduct for using KutumbTree. We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and secure platform for all users. These guidelines ensure KutumbTree remains a positive community where everyone can preserve and share their family history.

2. What You May Do

✓ Allowed Activities:

  • Create and manage your own family trees
  • Add information about your family members (with their consent)
  • Upload family photos and documents
  • Invite family members to collaborate
  • Share your family tree with trusted family members
  • Export your family tree data
  • Provide feedback and report bugs
  • Customize your privacy settings
  • Use the app for personal genealogy research
  • Archive or delete your own data

3. What You Must NOT Do

✗ Prohibited Activities:

  • Harassment & Abuse: Do not harass, bully, threaten, or abuse other users
  • Impersonation: Do not pretend to be someone else or misrepresent your identity
  • Unauthorized Access: Do not attempt to access accounts or data you don't own
  • Data Scraping: Do not use bots or automated tools to extract data
  • Malware: Do not upload or distribute viruses, malware, or malicious code
  • Spam: Do not send unsolicited messages or spam invitations
  • False Information: Do not deliberately upload false or misleading genealogical data
  • Copyright Violation: Do not upload copyrighted materials without permission
  • Illegal Content: Do not upload content that violates laws or regulations
  • Privacy Violation: Do not share others' data without their consent
  • System Abuse: Do not attempt to hack, compromise, or disrupt the service
  • Excessive Use: Do not overload servers with excessive API requests

4. Content Standards

Appropriate Content

  • Family photos suitable for mixed ages
  • Biographical information and historical records
  • Family milestones and achievements
  • Historical documents and census records
  • Family stories and narratives

Inappropriate Content

  • Explicit, adult, or pornographic content
  • Hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Violence or gore
  • Graphic or disturbing imagery
  • Content that defames or slanders individuals
Guidelines for Minor's Information:
  • Share children's data only in appropriate genealogical contexts
  • Obtain parental consent before sharing children's photos
  • Use privacy controls to limit visibility of minor's information
  • Never share children's location data or personal identifying information unnecessarily

5. Consent & Privacy

Before Sharing Others' Information

You agree to:

  • Obtain consent from family members before sharing their information
  • Respect others' wishes if they ask you to remove their data
  • Only invite family members via their correct contact information
  • Explain the purpose and scope of your family tree
  • Respect cultural and religious sensitivities

Sensitive Information

Be careful when sharing:

  • Health information or medical history
  • Financial information
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Contact information without consent
  • Information about adopted family members

6. Respect for Others

Respectful Behavior

  • Treat other users with courtesy and respect
  • Disagree constructively about genealogical information
  • Respect different family structures and relationships
  • Acknowledge cultural differences in naming and genealogy
  • Never discriminate based on race, religion, gender, or identity

Resolving Conflicts

If conflicts arise in a shared family tree:

  • Discuss disagreements respectfully
  • Provide sources for genealogical information
  • Use discussion features rather than direct edits
  • Contact support@kutumbtree.com if conflicts escalate

7. Account Responsibility

Your Account

You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your password confidential
  • Notifying us of unauthorized access
  • All activities under your account
  • Accurate account information

Multiple Accounts

While you may create multiple accounts:

  • Do not use multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions
  • Do not use multiple accounts to harass or spam others
  • Do not create fake accounts impersonating others

8. Violations & Enforcement

What Happens if You Violate These Rules

Violations may result in:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Temporary Suspension: Repeated violations (24 hours to 30 days)
  • Permanent Suspension: Severe or repeated violations
  • Account Deletion: Data removal at your request after suspension
  • Legal Action: For illegal activities

Appeal Process

If your account is suspended, you may appeal by contacting:

support@kutumbtree.com with subject: "Account Appeal"

We will review your appeal within 7 business days.

9. Reporting Violations

Found Something Wrong?

Report violations or concerns to:

  • Email: support@kutumbtree.com
  • Subject: "Report a Violation"
  • Include: Specific details and user accounts involved

What We Can Help With

  • Harassment or threats
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
  • Unauthorized access attempts
  • Copyright or intellectual property violations
  • Privacy breaches or data misuse

Response Time: We review reports within 24 hours.

10. System Use Guidelines

Technical Fair Use

  • API Limits: Maximum 100 requests per minute per account
  • File Uploads: Maximum 100 MB per file, 1 GB per user
  • Concurrent Logins: Maximum 5 concurrent sessions per account
  • Automated Access: Bots and scrapers not permitted

If Limits Are Exceeded

Your account may be temporarily restricted. To restore access:

  • Reduce API requests
  • Delete unnecessary files
  • Contact support@kutumbtree.com to request higher limits

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. You will be notified of material changes via:

  • Email notification
  • In-app notice
  • Updated date on this page

Continued use of KutumbTree indicates acceptance of updated policies.
