🍪 KutumbTree Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how KutumbTree uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you and enhance your experience. Please read this policy carefully to understand our cookie practices.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website or use our app. They help us remember information about you, such as your login status and preferences.

Types of Cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by KutumbTree
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by other websites (minimal use)

3. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
auth_token Authentication & login persistence 24 hours Session
refresh_token Session renewal for continuous use 7 days Persistent
user_preferences Store your UI preferences 1 year Persistent
theme_preference Remember light/dark mode choice 1 year Persistent
language_preference Store your language preference 1 year Persistent

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

We use minimal analytics to understand how you use KutumbTree and improve the service:

  • Usage Analytics: Track feature usage and user flows
  • Error Tracking: Log errors for debugging and improvement
  • Performance Metrics: Monitor load times and responsiveness
Privacy First: All analytics are anonymized and do not identify you personally. You can disable analytics in your privacy settings.

Functional Cookies

  • CSRF Protection: Security token to prevent cross-site attacks
  • Session Management: Maintain your login session securely

Marketing Cookies

We DO NOT use marketing or tracking cookies to follow you across the internet.

4. Similar Technologies

Beyond cookies, we may use other technologies for similar purposes:

  • Local Storage: Store user preferences locally on your device
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage for current session only
  • Web Beacons: Minimal use for analytics (can be disabled)

5. Third-Party Cookies

We minimize use of third-party services. If we use them, they include:

  • Hosting Provider (Railway): May set performance cookies
  • CDN Services: May set caching and delivery cookies

We do NOT use cookies from:

  • Google Analytics or other tracking services
  • Social media platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Any third-party marketing tools

6. Your Cookie Choices

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete specific cookies or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Set privacy levels for cookie acceptance

In-App Controls

KutumbTree provides cookie management options in:

  • Settings → Privacy: Control analytics and non-essential cookies
  • Settings → Data: View and manage stored preferences

Disabling Cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality. The app requires authentication cookies to work properly.

7. Cookie Consent

When you first use KutumbTree, you may see a cookie consent banner. This banner allows you to:

  • Accept All: Accept all cookies including analytics
  • Essential Only: Use only essential cookies needed for security
  • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to allow
  • Reject All: Decline non-essential cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time in the app's privacy settings.

8. Cookies and Security

Our security measures include:

  • Secure Flag: Cookies only sent over HTTPS
  • HttpOnly Flag: Cookies inaccessible to JavaScript (prevents XSS attacks)
  • SameSite Attribute: Protection against CSRF attacks
  • Encrypted Tokens: Authentication cookies contain encrypted, signed tokens

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices evolve. We will notify you of any material changes via:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Updated notice in the app
  • Updated date on this page

Your continued use of KutumbTree after updates constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
